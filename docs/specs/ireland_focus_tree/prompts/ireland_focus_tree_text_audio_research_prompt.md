# Ireland Focus Tree Text and Audio Research Prompt

Feature slug: `ireland_focus_tree`

This is a research handoff. It does not write final localisation, edit gameplay, wire sound or create visual assets.

## Quote and remark research packages

Research twelve packages:

1. constitutional drafting, Dáil procedure, President, Seanad and coalition language
2. neutrality, emergency law, censorship, internment, weather and covert liaison
3. Labour, trade unions, housing, wages, transport and parliamentary social policy
4. Clann na Talmhan, smallholders, cooperatives, land, roads and rural politics
5. IRA leadership, Army Council, prisoners, Civil War memory and partition
6. Republican Congress, anti-sectarian work, common programme and social republicanism
7. O'Duffy, the National Corporate Party, Spain, corporatism and succession
8. Ailtirí programmes, Irish-language total state, publications, antisemitism and postwar split
9. Church institutions, Catholic social thought, minority-faith language and Vatican diplomacy
10. Northern unionist, nationalist, labour, industrial, local-government and wartime language
11. Gaelic revival, folklore, royal sites, provincial institutions, Brehon material and public cultural work
12. Otherworld, heroic-cycle, obligation, stewardship and impossible-route terminology

For every direct quotation or recognisable remark, record exact text, author or speaker, source work or archive, date, source URL, source type, attribution confidence, public-domain or copyright status and intended surface. Compare several candidates before recommending one. Reject unsourced quote-site wording and invented traditional sayings.

## Localisation direction

Return terminology and tone guidance, not pasteable event prose. Distinguish constitutional, Labour, republican, corporate, clerical, Ailtirí, Northern, royal, provincial and Otherworld voices. Keep authoritarian coercion, antisemitism, repression and exclusion explicit. Hidden text begins with evidence, testimony and consequences and does not announce the route.

## Dynamic content

Identify terms that must vary by office-holder, party, coalition, law, region, sponsor, value band, incident, settlement model, integration stage and obligation. Do not recommend text that exposes internal IDs or unrevealed route names.

## Optional audio briefs

Research only three audio roles:

- coastwatch and Atlantic ambience for a dedicated maritime or National Conditions surface
- constitutional or sacral High Kingship ceremony cue
- verified Otherworld entry cue

Audio is optional. Consider composition and recording rights separately. Prefer public-domain, Creative Commons, official archive or clearly licensed material. Record title, creator, performer, source, licence, attribution, duration, edit plan, original path and proposed final OGG path. No route or achievement depends on audio.
