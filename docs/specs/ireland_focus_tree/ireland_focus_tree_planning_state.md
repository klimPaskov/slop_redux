## Hidden path disposition

Hidden paths are accepted planned content. Earlier notes that treated hidden cultural restoration as queued or optional are superseded by part 10.

Accepted hidden families and overlays include civic cultural restoration, Emergency directorate, Atlantic neutral compact, common platform Northern settlement, corrupted restoration failure, compromised republican network, cross border Labour Council, constitutional backchannel, corporate chambers without O'Duffy, republican reconciliation backchannel, neutral aftershock recovery, and Northern emergency protectorate.

## BOP disposition

The Ireland BOP is accepted planned content. It improves the existing implementation by adding one visible internal authority layer with route specific active side pairs. It does not replace Constitutional Authority, Emergency Preparedness, Partition Pressure, or Foreign Access Pressure.

## Event disposition

Major events are accepted planned content. Earlier wording that made events sound conditional is superseded. The major event suite is mandatory for route choices, focus milestones, decision and mission outcomes, BOP thresholds, foreign reactions, Northern settlement, formables, hidden paths, AI runtime, and cleanup.

Flavour events are accepted planned content. The part 19 to part 21 layer adds 169 working handles or merged dynamic equivalents. These events must make Ireland feel historically grounded and lived in through civic life, press, schools, language, sport, rationing, fuel, shipping, local industry, rural society, labour, church and civil society, border life, diaspora, route micro flavour, and postwar memory. Each row requires a concrete gameplay effect and row level disposition.
