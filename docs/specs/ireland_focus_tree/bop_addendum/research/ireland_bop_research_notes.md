# Ireland BOP research notes

## Research use

The BOP is grounded in the same historical design anchors as the canonical Ireland package. It does not add new unresearched route claims. It turns the existing political tensions into one visible game layer.

## Constitutional authority anchor

The BOP first side in opening mode is supported by the 1937 constitutional settlement and the centrality of the Oireachtas and Dáil Éireann. The source basis supports a legal government side without turning it into generic stability.

Design use:

- Dáil and constitutional route choices push the BOP toward legal government.
- Legal dominance should help peaceful settlement and foreign trust.
- Legal dominance can still overcentralize if the player ignores emergency or social pressures.

## Emergency authority anchor

The Emergency Powers Act 1939 supports the emergency apparatus side. The Act concerned public safety, preservation of the state, public order, and control of supplies and services. That supports a BOP route where emergency command can defend the state but can also overreach.

Design use:

- Emergency Orders, rationing, censorship, port security, G2, and LDF centralization push toward emergency apparatus.
- Parliamentary review and civilian rule timetable push back.
- Extreme emergency side opens Emergency Directorate.

## Blueshirt and Fine Gael split anchor

Eoin O'Duffy and the Blueshirt legacy support a separate guard side in the BOP, while Fine Gael legalism and parliamentary opposition support a different democratic path. The BOP must keep these distinct.

Design use:

- Fine Gael route uses independent review versus defence liaison, not Blueshirt guard.
- Blueshirt route uses chambers versus guard.
- Guard extreme creates coup or civil resistance risk.

## Labour anchor

William Norton and the Labour Party route support a Labour cabinet side, while trade union and worker institution pressure supports a workers congress side. This lets Labour remain democratic socialist while still allowing a social republic route.

Design use:

- Cabinet Labour is stable reform.
- Workers congress is stronger reform and worker defence.
- Congress extreme creates production, church, employer, and foreign dependency crises.

## IRA and foreign contact anchor

The IRA route and Plan Kathleen style risk support the Army Council side and the compromised network failure path. The BOP should make hard republican tools faster but unstable.

Design use:

- Civilian republican front is political and safer.
- Army Council unlocks hard tools.
- German contact drives exposure, dependency, and crisis. It does not provide clean power.

## Northern settlement anchor

Partition Pressure remains the Northern mechanic. The BOP affects domestic legitimacy and coercion. Peaceful settlement wants legal, civic, Labour cabinet, or balanced bands. Coercive tools work faster but raise unionist alarm and British reaction.

## Asset source notes

Real historical flags, state symbols, party symbols, paramilitary insignia, and real leader portraits require source review. This BOP package mainly needs symbolic icons, so generated art is suitable unless the implementation chooses real symbols.

## Text and audio note

No quote, slogan, lyric fragment, motto, or audio choice is required for the BOP. If the implementation wants any real slogan, exact cultural phrase, or route motto, it must go through text and audio research first.
