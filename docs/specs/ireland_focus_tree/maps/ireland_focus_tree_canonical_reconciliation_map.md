# Route-to-conclusion rule

Every durable AI profile has one of four roles in the route coverage matrix:

1. a sovereign route with its own standard conclusion family
2. a support programme that qualifies a lawful host-government conclusion
3. a recovery profile that restores a valid host conclusion after exceptional government closes
4. the composite convergence order, which requires completed component conclusions and an additional consolidation sequence

A support programme cannot replace the head of government merely by becoming active. A recovery profile cannot erase misconduct or route history. A cosmetic name or flag cannot satisfy a conclusion.

# Historical classification rule

| Classification | Meaning | Implementation consequence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `H` | documented institution, actor, event, dispute, or baseline policy | historical AI may use it within documented conditions |
| `P` | plausible divergence rooted in documented actors, institutions, pressures, or strategic problems | requires named political, organisational, material, and campaign gates |
| `A` | deliberate absurdity after a valid hidden reveal | requires game-rule permission, serious cultural capacity, unusual campaign conditions, evidence, disqualifier checks, and explicit transition from `H` through `P` to `A` |

Otherworld containment remains `P` while the state is managing rumours, fraud, dangerous sites, or uncertain evidence. It becomes `A` only after verified impossible contact changes the mechanic and evidence ledger.

# Territorial and Northern rule

Claims, control, occupation, settlement, integration, and cores are separate states. Northern territory never transfers through a free focus reward. A Northern core requires integration stage `5` and the final regional review of rights, representation, services, policing, resistance, finance, guarantees, and demobilisation.

# Asset and research rule

Real people, historical flags, attested symbols, archives, documents, and real photographed scenes use sourced mode. Alternate, fictional, symbolic, supernatural, and impossible identities may use generated mode. Catalogue discovery is not clearance. Every sourced item carries provenance, rights, clearance, credit, rejected alternatives, source path, processed path, final path, and intended sprite metadata.

# Completion rule

The package is a canonical release candidate until the required `hoi4_improvement_loop_planner` run is completed with `fork_context=false`, its report is saved, every finding receives a disposition, accepted repairs are integrated into every affected canonical file, and validation is rerun. No manual review can be represented as that missing execution.
