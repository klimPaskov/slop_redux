# Northern event map

Northern events always distinguish unionist, nationalist, labour, British, local-government, industrial, security, religious, and civic actors. A British event cannot stand in for a unionist event. A Dublin claim event cannot stand in for a nationalist consent event.

The Northern chain includes confidence measures, policing incidents, border families, labour disputes, shipyard contracts, school funding, housing, local boundaries, plebiscite administration, conventions, British negotiations, war, occupation, disarmament, services, finance, guarantees, integration stages, resistance, core review, and postwar review.

# Concealed-content event map

Serious cultural events remain available without a hidden route. Reveal events record evidence class, provenance, public knowledge, disqualifiers, false leads, sites, institutions, and unusual campaign conditions.

A false lead ends through archive, legal, political, fraud, panic, or scholarly resolution. It does not delete language, folklore, site, regional, or archive benefits.

High Kingship, Five Provinces, and Otherworld events show the transition from `H` institutions to `P` restoration and then `A` sovereignty. The first impossible event cannot appear before the evidence and campaign gates are complete.

# Postwar and conclusion event map

Postwar events review emergency authority, supply controls, mobilisation, veterans, merchant shipping, reconstruction, republic status, Commonwealth relations, UN application, aid, alignment, Northern settlement, regional orders, and route identity.

Every route conclusion generates:

1. eligibility review
2. blocked-surface report when needed
3. institutional settlement
4. domestic reaction
5. foreign reaction where relevant
6. country identity activation
7. 180-day consolidation mission
8. success, partial success, or failure
9. final cleanup and archive

# Event asset and text handoff

Part 6 receives an event-family asset class rather than a final image selection.

- Real dated events, leaders, ports, documents, bombing, ships, sites, and institutions use sourced archival research.
- Fictional alternate scenes, regime reveals, impossible councils, supernatural sites, and symbolic route conclusions use generated art.
- Event families that justify quotes, period slogans, public-domain literary material, cultural remarks, or audio are flagged for research. Part 5 does not invent them.
- Major public changes can use news images. Domestic administration and lived consequences normally use report images.
- Scripted GUI and animation are justified only where they improve management of the National Settlement, Provision, Readiness, Northern integration, or concealed evidence.

# Part 6 handoff

Part 6 consolidates AI reactions, event option priorities, achievement tracking, image and icon ledgers, animation, quote, cultural remark, audio, and localisation direction. It cannot reduce the event volume floor, remove continuous flavour, merge actor families, or turn major chains into single popups.
