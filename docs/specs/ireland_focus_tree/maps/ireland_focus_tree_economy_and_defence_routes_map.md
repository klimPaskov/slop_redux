# National Provision map

## National Provision opening calculation

The displayed value is the rounded arithmetic mean of four components.

| Component | Opening | Core evidence |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Food Security | `55` | output, inputs, imports, storage, prices, nutrition, local distribution |
| Energy Continuity | `25` | coal, oil, turf, peat, electricity, storage, generation, allocation |
| Transport Serviceability | `40` | rail, roads, rolling stock, vehicles, depots, maintenance, local delivery |
| Maritime Access | `30` | tonnage, crews, ports, charters, repair, routes, insurance, foreign access |

```text
55 + 25 + 40 + 30 = 150
150 / 4 = 37.5
Displayed National Provision = 38
```

Industrial output is derived from the four components and from skills, standards, machinery, labour organisation, capital, and contracts. It is not a fifth component.

## Provision bands

| Range | State | Operational meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `0` to `19` | Systemic Breakdown | national distribution and essential services cannot sustain ordinary government or military policy |
| `20` to `34` | Acute Scarcity | severe rationing and improvised allocation keep the state functioning |
| `35` to `49` | Strained Provision | hard priority choices and high exposure to harvest, energy, transport, and shipping shocks |
| `50` to `64` | Managed Supply | limited shocks can be absorbed and ordinary programmes can be delivered |
| `65` to `79` | Resilient Provision | prolonged defence and reconstruction are sustainable |
| `80` to `100` | Strategic Capacity | exceptional national and regional programmes become possible |

## National Provision bottleneck rules

```text
Any component below 20
  -> National Provision cap 35

Two or more components below 30
  -> National Provision cap 45

Any component at 0
  -> national emergency

National Provision below 35
  -> prolonged mobilisation gains capped
```

Actions can require both a Provision floor and a named component floor. A high mean cannot prove that shipping, energy, food, or internal delivery exists.
