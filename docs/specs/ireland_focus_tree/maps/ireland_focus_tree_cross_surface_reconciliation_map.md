# AI reconciliation

The authoritative AI ledger contains exactly 30 durable profiles and ten temporary state overlays. The foreign reaction ledger contains exactly twelve actor families and twelve primary response action classes.

| AI layer | Count | Purpose | Cannot do |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| durable profiles | `30` | hold long-term route identity, target bands, category priorities, diplomacy, Northern policy, war posture, recovery and conclusion intent | replace route gates with weights or erase prior failures |
| temporary overlays | `10` | handle election campaign, government formation, national emergency, Provision collapse, invasion or war, Northern occupation and integration, sponsor collapse, cultural investigation and reveal, postwar transition, and route review | become a new government route or reset route history |
| foreign actor AI | `12` families | calculate separate reactions through the 14-day window | merge leverage or promise an undeliverable action |
| concealed AI permissions | route-specific | keep historical commitment at zero and enable rare valid alternatives | infer sovereignty from cultural work or bypass evidence |

AI consumes the same Action Burden, costs, mission caps, reserved slots, partial-result rules, offices, laws, obligations, values and map objectives as the player. It can accept a mapped partial success. It cannot invent a success state.

# Cross-surface acceptance tests

1. Every route profile has a conclusion, support role, recovery role or composite role in the route coverage matrix.
2. Every event family writes to a real mechanic, objective, lifecycle, relationship or memory state.
3. Every achievement condition has a planned tracking source.
4. Every visible route identity has a flag, leader or institutional portrait, focus and decision art, spirit and law art, event art and text direction as required.
5. Every foreign response remains actor-specific and deliverable.
6. Every hidden surface preserves secrecy until the mapped reveal.
7. The exact counts in the six-part package remain unchanged after reconciliation.
8. No prompt duplicates ownership or grants fallback permission.
