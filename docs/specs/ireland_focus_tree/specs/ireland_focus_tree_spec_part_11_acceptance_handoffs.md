## Hidden path disposition

Hidden paths are accepted planned content. The earlier queued status for cultural restoration is superseded by part 10.

Accepted hidden families and overlays:

- civic cultural restoration
- Emergency directorate
- Atlantic neutral compact as a conference or faction concept
- common platform Northern settlement
- corrupted restoration failure branch
- compromised republican network
- cross border Labour Council and popular defence front
- constitutional backchannel
- corporate chambers without O'Duffy
- republican reconciliation backchannel
- neutral aftershock recovery
- Northern emergency protectorate

The Atlantic compact remains a conference or limited faction system. It is not a formable country. Civic cultural restoration remains constitutional, educational, diplomatic, and non monarchical. Aiséirghe inspired material is a rare dangerous capture or failure pressure that requires source review and severe instability.

## Anti bloat rules

Do not add more ordinary political routes after this package unless they solve a specific gameplay gap. The current route set covers sovereign constitutionalism, legal opposition, democratic socialism, radical socialist pressure, corporatism, underground republicanism, active neutrality, Northern settlement, hidden civic and emergency paths, and late game diplomacy.

Do not add a separate church government route without a full political, country package, AI, asset, and decision design. The Vatican is a mediation and legitimacy actor in this package, not a military or governing sponsor.

Do not add an instant empire, high kingship state, pan Celtic formable, or Atlantic country tag. Unified Ireland is the main identity change. The Atlantic compact is diplomatic. Cultural restoration is civic.
