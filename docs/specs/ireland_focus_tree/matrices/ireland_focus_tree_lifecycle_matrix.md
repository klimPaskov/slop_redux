Planning only. This matrix contains no implementation code or final localisation. Lifecycle states preserve the `H`, `P`, and `A` classification inherited from the route specifications. Any `A` state requires the recorded transition from documented institutions through plausible restoration into deliberate absurdity.

# Lifecycle acceptance rules

1. Every office has entry, vacancy, succession, replacement and cleanup.
2. Every party and movement has a dated foundation, organisation state, legal status, leader, seats or network strength, split and dissolution state.
3. Every law has entry, review, escalation, abuse, replacement, expiry and restoration.
4. Every persistent idea belongs to one of three route-spirit families.
5. Every temporary idea has an expiry or replacement.
6. Claims, control, occupation, settlement, integration and cores remain separate.
7. Foreign access, guarantees, subjects and factions record scope, duration, command, obligations, exit and cleanup.
8. A route conclusion cannot fire while a required lifecycle is unresolved.
