# Ireland Focus Tree Achievement Matrix

Feature slug: `ireland_focus_tree`

Status: Part 6 authoritative achievement ledger

All achievement names are working labels. Final player-facing wording belongs to implementation.

# Global tracking contract

- The player must start as Ireland in the 1 January 1936 start. Cosmetic identity changes do not disqualify because the underlying country remains the original campaign country.
- Country switching, release and takeover of another tag, debug completion, or a route-start shortcut disqualifies every achievement in this package.
- Every qualifying minimum or maximum is stored as campaign history. Reaching a good value at the end does not erase an earlier disqualifying dependency, atrocity, fabrication, unlawful office or broken obligation.
- Achievements that require no client status track the maximum of every source-specific leverage value and every dependency stage.
- Northern achievements require the named settlement process, stage 5 integration and the final regional review. Control, occupation or a blanket core event cannot satisfy them.
- Hidden achievements begin invisible. They become visible only at the recorded reveal stage, except the convergence achievement which remains hidden until completion.
- A route conclusion alone never unlocks an achievement unless every listed material, legal, office, foreign, Northern, cleanup and anti-exploit condition also passes.

# Visibility and notification rules

- Visible achievements show broad public goals and current blockers without revealing secret future routes.
- A hidden achievement can reveal its working title and visible criteria only after the route reveal. Its unseen preconditions remain in the evidence system.
- Failure notification occurs only when the failure is irreversible. Temporary value drops do not announce permanent failure unless the achievement records an extrema condition.
- Achievement progress never exposes hidden AI weights, fabricated evidence checks or an unrevealed route name.

# Achievement acceptance tests

1. The roster contains exactly 32 entries.
2. No entry unlocks from route selection or a single focus.
3. Every entry has a start-country rule, tracking record and disqualifier set.
4. The four hidden mastery entries require full reveal and cannot be earned through fabricated evidence.
5. Northern entries require stage 5 and final review.
6. No-dependency entries use campaign maxima, not end-state values.
7. Authoritarian-route entries do not reward an atrocity, antisemitic escalation or foreign client state.
8. Each icon has normal, grey and not-eligible variants.
