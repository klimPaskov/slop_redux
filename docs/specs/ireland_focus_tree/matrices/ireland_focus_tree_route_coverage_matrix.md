# Ireland Focus Tree Route Coverage Matrix

Feature slug: `ireland_focus_tree`

Status: Part 7 canonical route registry

All names are working labels. `H` is documented historical baseline, `P` is plausible divergence, and `A` is deliberate absurdity after a valid reveal. This matrix does not replace the detailed route specifications. It gives implementation one registry connecting each durable AI profile to its route family, conclusion, achievements, identity package, and primary source files.

## Coverage rules

- Exactly 30 durable AI profiles remain authoritative.
- Exactly ten temporary overlays modify a profile without replacing it.
- A support profile may conclude through a lawful host-government conclusion when it does not create a separate sovereign regime.
- Serious public culture remains useful under every route through the current host profile and its normal Culture category priority. AI-12 is reserved for a government that makes serious cultural stewardship its primary national programme.
- Final convergence is the composite highest variant of the Otherworld and convergence conclusion family. It does not create a sixteenth conclusion-image family.
- A cosmetic name or flag cannot activate a route conclusion.
- Every conclusion must resolve government, law, Provision, Readiness, foreign obligations, Northern policy, emergency authority, demobilisation, identity, and cleanup.

## Coverage acceptance

1. Every AI profile has a route family, valid commitment state, conclusion or host conclusion, achievement link, identity rule, and source path.
2. Support profiles do not create free sovereign outcomes.
3. Every authoritarian and impossible route retains its coercion, exclusion, dependency, rights, supply, and cleanup costs.
4. Every concealed route remains zero-weight for historical AI until its exact permission and reveal gates are satisfied.
5. Northern control, settlement, integration, and cores remain separate for every profile.
6. The matrix does not weaken any detailed gate in Parts 2 through 6.
