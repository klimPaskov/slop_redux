# Ireland Emergency Readiness Seal Frame Plan

| Frame | Motion state | Visual change | Prompt delta | Anchor note | Loop note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 000 | low alert | brass emergency bell, steel helmet, sandbags, and dim lantern inside Irish green-gold seal | lowest lantern light, shutters nearly closed | center | start state |
| 001 | alert rising | lantern shutters open slightly, bell clapper begins to move | faint amber light on lower rim | center | easing in |
| 002 | mobilizing | bell angled forward, helmet catches more light, papers tucked behind sandbags | stronger amber light and dust motes | center | rising |
| 003 | peak warning | bell forward and lantern brightest, sandbag rim strongly lit | strongest amber core, crisp silhouette | center | peak |
| 004 | controlled readiness | bell settles, lantern still bright, rim highlights even | focused amber beam inside seal | center | fall begins |
| 005 | watch rotation | lantern shutters begin closing, helmet shadow deepens | glow recedes toward center | center | falling |
| 006 | alert easing | bell returns upright, only small amber rim remains | dim warm highlights | center | near loop |
| 007 | low alert return | same low-alert composition with tiny remaining light | visually close to frame 000 | center | closes loop |
